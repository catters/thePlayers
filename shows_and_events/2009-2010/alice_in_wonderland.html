By Lewis Carroll
Adapted by William Glennon

All of the fun and nonsense of the classic lewis Carroll story for the whole family to enjoy!

The Phire Phlies, The Phoenix Players youth acting ensemble with actors aged 7-13, will present a new adaptation of the story by playwright William Glennon. This version is so close to the original in intention and feeling that audiences might suspect that Carroll himself had written it. In this adaptation, a group of performers find themselves unable to perform the play because there is no one willing to play Alice. Magically, however, Alice herself appears, ready for the adventure to begin. With innovative staging designed to appeal especially to children, the wit, nonsense, and fun of Lewis Carroll’s delightful book is presented in a way that makes the story enjoyable for even the youngest playgoer.
