Dilemmas with Dinner

By Robin Roberts

Synopsis

A hysterical comedy by one of America's new and talented playwrights.

Junior executive Brooke invites her boss and his wife over for dinner in the hopes of securing a promotion.

While frantically getting ready, Brooke and her husband discover that the hired help is coincidentally the boss' ex-daughter-in-law. Just when it seems everything that could go wrong has gone wrong, Brooke's boss has an unfortunate announcement to make - though not as unfortunate as the candlestick attack, appetizer toss, and general chaos that then ensues.

It's farce at its best.

Directed by Tim Redlinger
