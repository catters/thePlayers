The New Adventures of Dorothy in Oz

By Jeff Duncan

Synopsis

Is it deja vu all over again?

Dorothy is back in Oz, but things are just a bit different than when she last ended up there.

Is Glinda the Good Witch really upset because she didn't get the main role in the Broadway production of Wicked?

And what's with the major construction project on The Yellow Brick Road?

All is not as it was, as Airplane-type humor meets Dorothy and Oz. A fun show for adults and children being premiered by The Phoenix Players and The Phoenix Players Phire Phlies in South Lyon.
