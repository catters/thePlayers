Scrooge has Left the Building

By The Phire Phlies Youth Ensemble

Synopsis

It’s Midnight on Christmas Eve (or is it Christmas Eve Eve?) so Marley heads off (as usual) with his pals to scare the nightcap off Scrooge; but wait...

  • What was the address again?
  • Who is this guy living in Scrooge’s house--or is it Scrooge’s house?
  • Did anyone check the GPS?

It’s the old tale with a comic twist. Come and enjoy a hilarious short break in your hectic Christmas season.
