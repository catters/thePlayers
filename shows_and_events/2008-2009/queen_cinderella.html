By Anne C. Levy

What happened after She Married the Prince?

  • Cat juggling?
  • Stealing other characters out of their fairy tales?
  • Making the lives of her "no-longer-wicked" stepsisters, Muffy and Buffy, a nightmare?

Can this possibly be the sweet girl we knew who married Prince Charming?

Sad, but true. It seems that power has gone to the head of Queen Cinderella, and she's making everyone's life miserable. What to do?

Why - find the fairy godmother and get some magic to make her bullying stop, of course - But on the way to her retirement home in Gnome Alaska, the stepsisters, Puss in Boots, Goldilocks, and Dorothy Gale from Kansas, soon discover that the journey isn't so easy.

Faced with a Troll guarding a Troll Road, Fox Snooze carrying on a "pond-sy" scheme, and a strange invisible threat, the friends must learn some basic lessons about dealing with bullies.

Will they find the Fairy Godmother?

Will she provide them with the magic they need? Will they finally be able to tame the Queen of Mean?

All will be revealed in another of the trademark Phoenix Players Phire Phlies plays: shows that teach important lessons with laughs and fun for children and adults alike.

A perfect play for a scout troop, church or parents group outing.
Don't be disappointed - make your ticket reservations now!
