Laura Ingalls Wilder: Voice of the Prairie

 

By Kathryn Schultz Miller

Synopsis

A heartwarming play about one of America's best-loved authors and the characters she created. If you enjoyed reading about or watching the Ingalls family, you'll be enchanted by this play that the whole family will enjoy.

Laura Ingalls Wilder's books come alive in this play about her life and the characters that came out of her experiences in the America of the 1800's. Combining history with scenes from the author's life and her books, this new play promises to be a big hit.

If you loved Little House on the Prairie, you'll love this delightful show.

Sell outs are expected, but season ticket holders are assured of a reserved seat for the show date of their choice.
